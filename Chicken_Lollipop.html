Chicken Lollipop

Elevate your dining experience with the playful and delectable Chicken Lollipop, a culinary creation that combines artistry with indulgence. Chicken wings are expertly frenched and transformed into lollipops, marinated in a flavorful mix of spices and herbs. Deep-fried to achieve a golden, crispy exterior, these succulent delights are not just visually appealing but also an exploration of taste and texture. Dip them into a zesty sauce, and you're transported into a world where every lollipop bite is a journey of pure gastronomic delight.  

    

Ingredients

  1. Chicken wings/drumettes
  2. Soy sauce
  3. Chili sauce
  4. Ginger-garlic paste
  5. Red chili powder
  6. Turmeric powder
  7. Cornflour
  8. All-purpose flour
  9. Egg
  10. Salt to taste
  11. Oil for frying
  12. Spring onions (for garnish)
  13. Sesame seeds (optional, for garnish)

Directions

  1. Clean and prepare the chicken wings by removing excess skin and meat from the bone end to create a lollipop shape.
  2. In a bowl, mix soy sauce, chili sauce, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt to make the marinade.
  3. Marinate the chicken lollipops in the mixture and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, allowing the flavors to penetrate the meat.
  4. In a separate bowl, prepare a batter by mixing cornflour, all-purpose flour, and a beaten egg. Add water as needed to achieve a smooth consistency.
  5. Heat oil in a deep frying pan or fryer.
  6. Dip each marinated chicken lollipop into the batter, ensuring it is well-coated.
  7. Fry the coated chicken lollipops in hot oil until they are golden brown and crispy.
  8. Remove the chicken lollipops from the oil and place them on paper towels to absorb excess oil.
  9. Garnish with chopped spring onions and sesame seeds (optional).
  10. Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce.

Enjoy these crispy and flavorful Chicken Lollipops as a delightful appetizer! Adjust the spice levels according to your preferences.
