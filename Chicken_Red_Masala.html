Chicken Red Masala

Enter the realm of vibrant flavors with Chicken Red Masala, a culinary masterpiece that hails from the heart of Indian cuisine. This dish is an embodiment of warmth and spice, where tender chicken pieces are bathed in a rich and aromatic red masala gravy. The carefully balanced blend of spices, from cumin to coriander, creates a depth of flavor that dances on the taste buds. With every spoonful, Chicken Red Masala beckons with its enticing hue and promises a journey through the rich tapestry of Indian culinary traditions.  

    

Ingredients

  1. Chicken pieces
  2. Onions, finely chopped
  3. Tomatoes, chopped
  4. Ginger-garlic paste
  5. Red chili powder
  6. Coriander powder
  7. Cumin powder
  8. Turmeric powder
  9. Garam masala
  10. Cinnamon stick
  11. Cardamom pods
  12. Cloves
  13. Bay leaves
  14. Curry leaves
  15. Fresh coriander leaves, chopped
  16. Green chilies, chopped
  17. Yogurt
  18. Cashew nuts (optional, for creaminess)
  19. Oil or ghee
  20. Salt to taste

Directions

  1. Heat oil or ghee in a pan. Add cinnamon stick, cardamom pods, cloves, bay leaves, and curry leaves. Sauté until fragrant.
  2. Add finely chopped onions and cook until golden brown.
  3. Stir in ginger-garlic paste and cook until the raw smell disappears.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft and the oil starts to separate.
  5. Introduce red chili powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, turmeric powder, and garam masala. Cook the spices for a few minutes to enhance their flavors.
  6. Add chicken pieces to the pan and sear until they are well-coated and slightly browned.
  7. Whisk yogurt and add it to the pan, stirring continuously to avoid curdling.
  8. If using, add cashew nuts to the mixture for creaminess.
  9. Cover and simmer until the chicken is cooked through and the masala thickens.
  10. Garnish with chopped fresh coriander leaves and green chilies.
  11. Serve hot with rice or naan.

Enjoy your flavorful and spicy Chicken Red Masala! Adjust the spice levels according to your preferences.
