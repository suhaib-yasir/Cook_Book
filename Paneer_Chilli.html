Paneer Chilli

As you set foot into the realm of Paneer Chilli, you're greeted by a dish that seamlessly intertwines the creamy indulgence of paneer with the vibrant fervor of chili. Imagine succulent cubes of paneer, meticulously marinated to perfection, engaging in a lively dance with colorful bell peppers and onions. The magic unfolds in the sizzle of the wok, where the paneer absorbs the bold flavors of soy sauce, ginger, and garlic. Whether served dry, allowing the textures to shine, or enveloped in a luscious gravy, Paneer Chilli is a symphony of taste that beckons with the promise of a culinary voyage where Indian and Chinese culinary traditions harmoniously converge.  

    

Ingredients

  1. 200g paneer, cut into cubes
  2. 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  3. 1/4 cup cornflour
  4. 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  5. 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  6. Salt to taste
  7. Oil for frying
  8. 2 tablespoons oil
  9. 1 tablespoon finely chopped garlic
  10. 1 tablespoon finely chopped ginger
  11. 1 cup thinly sliced onions
  12. 1 cup thinly sliced bell peppers (mix of colors)
  13. 2-3 green chilies, slit
  14. 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  15. 1 tablespoon tomato ketchup
  16. 1 tablespoon chili sauce
  17. 1 tablespoon vinegar
  18. 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  19. 1/2 cup spring onion greens, chopped
  20. Sesame seeds for garnish

Directions

  1. In a bowl, mix all-purpose flour, cornflour, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, and salt. Coat paneer cubes with this mixture.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and fry the coated paneer cubes until golden brown. Set aside.
  3. In another pan, heat 2 tablespoons of oil. Sauté garlic and ginger until aromatic.
  4. Add sliced onions, bell peppers, and green chilies. Stir-fry until the vegetables are slightly tender.
  5. Combine soy sauce, tomato ketchup, chili sauce, vinegar, and sugar. Mix well and add to the vegetables.
  6. Add the fried paneer cubes and toss until everything is well-coated in the sauce.
  7. Garnish with spring onion greens and sesame seeds.
  8. Serve hot and savor the deliciousness of Paneer Chilli!

Enjoy the spicy and flavorful Paneer Chilli! Adjust the spice levels and sauce quantities according to your preference.
