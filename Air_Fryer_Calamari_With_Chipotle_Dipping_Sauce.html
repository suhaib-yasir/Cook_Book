Air Fryer Calamari With Chipotle Dipping Sauce

Dive into a culinary adventure with our Air Fryer Calamari, where the tantalizing combination of texture and flavor takes center stage. This dish presents a modern twist on the classic calamari, artfully air-fried to achieve a golden, crispy exterior that gives way to perfectly tender rings beneath. The magic doesn't stop there - the ensemble is elevated with a Chipotle Dipping Sauce. Picture the smoky allure of chipotle peppers harmonizing with the succulence of calamari, creating a dance of heat and crunch that beckons every food lover to embark on a delightful journey of taste.

Ingredients

  1. 1 pound frozen calamari, thawed, cleaned and thinly sliced into rings 1/4 inch wide, with the tentacles separately sliced into small pieces
  2. 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice
  3. 1.1/4 cups all-purpose flour (or a gluten-free alternative)
  4. 1 teaspoon paprika or Old Bay seasoning
  5. 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  6. 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  7. 1/2 cup milk
  8. 1 large egg
  9. Prepared creamy chipotle sauce, for dipping

Directions

  1. Pat the calamari dry with a paper towel. Place all the calamari into a medium mixing bowl and toss with lemon juice until well coated.
  2. Lightly oil Air Fryer basket (or coat basket with cooking spray); return basket to Air Fryer and preheat to 400°F.
  3. In another medium mixing bowl, combine flour, paprika (or Old Bay), salt, and pepper; mix well.
  4. In a third mixing bowl, whisk milk and egg until well combined.
  5. Dredge each piece of calamari first in the flour mixture, then in the egg mixture, and finally in the flour mixture again, until well coated.
  6. Place one layer of coated calamari in the air fryer basket. (Do not overlap calamari. If extra pieces remain, cook calamari in batches.) Coat calamari with cooking spray.
  7. Cook at 400°F for 8 minutes, or until calamari is cooked through.
  8. Serve immediately with a bowl of chipotle sauce for dipping.

Enjoy the crispy and flavorful Air Fryer Calamari with the smoky kick of Chipotle Dipping Sauce! Adjust the spice levels and ingredients to suit your taste
