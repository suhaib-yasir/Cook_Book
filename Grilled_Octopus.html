Grilled Octopus

Elevate your dining experience with the sophistication of Easy Grilled Octopus, where simplicity meets culinary finesse. The octopus, tenderized to perfection, undergoes a transformation on the grill, acquiring a tantalizing char on its exterior while maintaining a melt-in-the-mouth tenderness within. Marinated in a blend of olive oil, garlic, and fragrant herbs, this dish embodies the marriage of simplicity and refined taste. Served with a squeeze of lemon, each bite is a revelation - a culinary adventure that invites you to savor the ocean's bounty in a manner that is both approachable and elegantly nuanced.

Ingredients

  1. 2 pounds fresh octopus (baby, medium, or large)
  2. Olive oil
  3. 2 garlic cloves, roughly chopped
  4. Juice from 1 lemon
  5. Salt and pepper to taste
  6. 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  7. 2 tsp chopped fresh parsley

Directions

  1. Place octopus in a pot and cover with enough water. Bring to a boil. Boil for 40 minutes.
  2. Remove octopus from hot water, rinse, and place in a bowl. Drizzle with olive oil and add chopped garlic. Let it cool and rest at room temperature for 30 minutes to 1 hour.
  3. Preheat a gas grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Slice octopus tentacles. Grill for 3-4 minutes per side, until charred.
  5. Remove from heat and place in a bowl.
  6. Drizzle with olive oil and add lemon juice. Season with salt and pepper. Sprinkle some oregano (optional) and parsley on top. You can also add some chopped garlic, too (optional).
  7. Serve.

Enjoy the tender and flavorful Grilled Octopus with the brightness of lemon and the savory touch of garlic and oregano! Adjust the seasoning and cooking time based on your preferences and the size of the octopus.
