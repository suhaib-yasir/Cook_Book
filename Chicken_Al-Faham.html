Chicken Al-Faham

Allow your senses to embark on a journey of culinary ecstasy with Chicken Alfaham, a dish that transcends ordinary dining into an extraordinary experience. This Middle Eastern-inspired delicacy captivates with its tantalizing aroma and succulent tenderness. Marinated with a melange of aromatic spices and herbs, the chicken is grilled to perfection, resulting in a smoky and charred exterior that conceals a juicy, flavorful interior. The Alfaham experience is a symphony of tastes, where each bite tells a story of culinary finesse and a longing for more.  

    

Ingredients

  1. Chicken pieces
  2. Yogurt
  3. Ginger-garlic paste
  4. Green chilies, chopped
  5. Coriander powder
  6. Cumin powder
  7. Turmeric powder
  8. Red chili powder
  9. Garam masala
  10. Lemon juice
  11. Black pepper powder
  12. Salt to taste
  13. Mustard oil
  14. Cashew nuts (optional, for marination)
  15. Fresh coriander leaves, chopped (for garnish)

Directions

  1. Marinate the chicken pieces in a mixture of yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, chopped green chilies, coriander powder, cumin powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, lemon juice, black pepper powder, and salt. Optionally, you can add cashew nuts for extra richness. Let it marinate for at least 2 hours or overnight.
  2. Preheat the oven to a high temperature (around 200°C or 400°F).
  3. Place the marinated chicken on a baking tray and bake in the preheated oven until the chicken is cooked through and has a nice, golden-brown color.
  4. In a pan, heat mustard oil until it reaches its smoking point. Take it off the heat and let it cool slightly.
  5. Brush the baked chicken with the prepared mustard oil for added flavor.
  6. Garnish with freshly chopped coriander leaves.
  7. Serve hot with your favorite side dishes or accompaniments.

Enjoy the delicious flavors of Chicken Al-Faham! Adjust the spice levels and garnishes according to your preferences.
