Dragon Chicken

Dragon Chicken, an embodiment of culinary innovation, entices with the tantalizing fusion of succulent chicken and the bold flavors of Chinese spices. The chicken, meticulously marinated and wok-fried to achieve the perfect balance of crispiness and tenderness, becomes a canvas for the enchanting Dragon sauce. This luscious concoction unveils a symphony of sweet, spicy, and savory notes that dance on the palate, making Dragon Chicken an irresistible choice for those seeking a medley of bold flavors that ignite the taste buds and leave a lasting impression.

Ingredients

  1. Chicken, boneless and diced
  2. Corn flour
  3. Egg, beaten
  4. Soy sauce
  5. Chili sauce
  6. Ginger-garlic paste
  7. Onions, thinly sliced
  8. Green chilies, chopped
  9. Spring onions, chopped
  10. Oil for frying
  11. Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Marinate chicken with corn flour, beaten egg, soy sauce, chili sauce, ginger-garlic paste, salt, and pepper.
  2. Deep fry marinated chicken until golden brown and crisp.
  3. In a separate pan, saut√© onions, green chilies, and add fried chicken. Toss well.
  4. Garnish with chopped spring onions and serve hot.

Enjoy the savory and spicy flavor of homemade Dragon Chicken! Adjust the spice levels and sauce quantities according to your preference.
