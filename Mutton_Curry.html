Mutton Curry

Mutton Curry, a classic in the diverse tapestry of Indian cuisine, is a culinary journey that spans regions and generations. The dish is a symphony of textures and tastes, where succulent mutton pieces are marinated and slow-cooked to perfection in a tantalizing gravy. The marriage of aromatic spices, from cumin and coriander to cloves and cinnamon, creates a layered complexity that unfolds with every bite. Whether enjoyed with steamed rice or warm naan, Mutton Curry invites you to partake in a hearty and flavorful experience that resonates with the essence of traditional Indian cooking.  

    

Ingredients

  1. 500 grams mutton, cleaned and cut into pieces
  2. 2 large onions, finely sliced
  3. 2 tomatoes, chopped
  4. 2 tablespoons oil
  5. 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  6. 1/2 cup yogurt
  7. 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  8. 1 tablespoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  9. 1 tablespoon coriander powder
  10. 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  11. 1 teaspoon garam masala
  12. Salt to taste
  13. Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Directions

  1. Clean and wash the mutton pieces thoroughly.
  2. In a large pot or pressure cooker, heat oil over medium heat.
  3. Add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until the raw smell disappears.
  5. Introduce the cleaned mutton pieces and cook until they are well-seared on all sides.
  6. Stir in chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft and the oil starts to separate.
  7. Add mutton curry masala, red chili powder, turmeric powder, coriander powder, and salt. Mix well to coat the mutton pieces with the spices.
  8. Pour in enough water to cover the mutton and bring it to a boil. Reduce the heat, cover, and simmer until the mutton is tender. If using a pressure cooker, cook for the specified time.
  9. Once the mutton is cooked, check for seasoning and adjust if necessary.
  10. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with rice, naan, or your preferred choice of bread.

Enjoy the rich and flavorful Mutton Curry! Customize the spice levels based on your preferences.
