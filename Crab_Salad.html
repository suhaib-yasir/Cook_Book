Crab Salad

Our Crab Salad isn't just a dish; it's a celebration of freshness and vibrancy. Imagine a canvas adorned with lump crab meat, avocado slices, and crisp greens, all coming together in a mosaic of color and flavor. The ensemble is elevated by a zesty dressing that marries the sweet notes of crab with the invigorating tang of citrus. This salad isn't a mere medley of ingredients; it's a culinary composition that beckons health-conscious diners and seafood enthusiasts alike to indulge in a refreshing and wholesome symphony.

Ingredients

  1. 12 ounces canned lump crabmeat, rinsed and drained well
  2. 2 celery stalks, finely chopped
  3. 1 large avocado, diced or mashed
  4. 1/2 red bell pepper, finely chopped
  5. 2 tablespoons green onion, chopped
  6. 2 tablespoons artichoke hearts, chopped
  7. 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  8. 1 tablespoon olive oil
  9. 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  10. Salt and pepper, to taste

Directions

  1. Combine all ingredients except the salt and pepper and mix well. (For a creamier crab salad, you can use a food processor to mix).
  2. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Serve on top of mixed greens, with crackers and veggies as a dip, or on whole wheat pita as a sandwich.

Enjoy this delicious and simple Crab Salad that highlights the natural sweetness of crab meat! Adjust the seasoning and ingredients to suit your taste preferences.
