Butter Chicken

Indulge your taste buds in the rich and flavorful experience of our signature Butter Chicken. Savor the perfect blend of tender, succulent chicken pieces bathed in a luscious tomato-based curry, enriched with aromatic spices and finished with a generous dollop of creamy butter. Our Butter Chicken is a culinary masterpiece that captures the essence of North Indian cuisine, delivering a mouthwatering harmony of textures and tastes

Ingredients

  1. Chicken pieces
  2. Yogurt
  3. Ginger-garlic paste
  4. Garam masala
  5. Coriander powder
  6. Cumin powder
  7. Kashmiri red chili powder
  8. Turmeric powder
  9. Tomato puree
  10. Onions, finely chopped
  11. Butter
  12. Heavy cream
  13. Kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  14. Sugar
  15. Salt to taste
  16. Oil for cooking
  17. Fresh coriander leaves, chopped (for garnish)

Directions

  1. Marinate the chicken pieces in a mixture of yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, garam masala, coriander powder, cumin powder, Kashmiri red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight.
  2. In a pan, heat oil and saut√© finely chopped onions until golden brown.
  3. Add the marinated chicken to the pan and cook until the chicken is browned.
  4. Stir in tomato puree and cook until the oil starts to separate from the masala.
  5. Add butter, heavy cream, kasuri methi, sugar, and salt to the chicken. Simmer until the chicken is cooked through and the sauce reaches the desired consistency.
  6. Garnish with chopped fresh coriander leaves.
  7. Serve hot with naan, rice, or your favorite bread.

Enjoy your homemade Butter Chicken! Adjust the spice levels and creaminess according to your taste preferences.
