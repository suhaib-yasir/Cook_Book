Chicken Tandoori

Ignite your taste buds with the sizzling sensation of Chicken Tandoor, a dish that epitomizes the art of Indian grilling. Marinated in a blend of yogurt, spices, and aromatic herbs, the chicken undergoes a magical metamorphosis in the intense heat of the tandoor. The result? Juicy, smoky, and charred perfection that captivates both the eyes and the palate. The tantalizing play of spices, including cumin, coriander, and garam masala, infuses the chicken with layers of flavor. Each succulent bite is a testament to the time-honored tradition of tandoori cooking, making Chicken Tandoor not just a dish but a celebration of culinary craftsmanship that beckons you to relish the thrill of the grill.  

    

Ingredients

  1. Chicken pieces
  2. Yogurt
  3. Ginger-garlic paste
  4. Tandoori masala
  5. Red chili powder
  6. Turmeric powder
  7. Garam masala
  8. Lemon juice
  9. Vegetable oil
  10. Salt to taste
  11. Coriander leaves (for garnish)

Directions

  1. Clean and wash the chicken pieces.
  2. In a bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, tandoori masala, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, lemon juice, vegetable oil, and salt to make the marinade.
  3. Coat the chicken pieces with the marinade, ensuring each piece is well-covered. Marinate for at least 2-4 hours, or overnight for best results.
  4. Preheat the oven to a high temperature (around 475°F or 245°C).
  5. Thread the marinated chicken pieces onto skewers and place them on a baking tray lined with foil.
  6. Bake the chicken in the preheated oven until it is cooked through and has a charred, tandoori finish. This usually takes about 25-30 minutes.
  7. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
  8. Serve hot with naan or rice and your favorite chutney or sauce.

Enjoy the authentic flavors of Chicken Tandoori, whether cooked in the oven or on the grill! Adjust the spice levels according to your preferences.
