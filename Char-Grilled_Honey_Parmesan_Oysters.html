Char-Grilled Honey Parmesan Oysters

Immerse yourself in the world of gourmet indulgence with our Char-Grilled Honey Parmesan Oysters. Fresh oysters, kissed by the flames of the grill, boast a tantalizing smokiness that intertwines seamlessly with the natural sweetness of honey. The addition of Parmesan introduces a savory complexity, transforming each bite into a sensory symphony. This dish is not merely a celebration of oceanic flavors it's a culinary masterpiece that invites connoisseurs to experience the sublime elegance of char-grilled perfection.

Ingredients

  1. 1 dozen large oysters, shucked with half-shell left on
  2. 1/2 cup olive oil mayonnaise
  3. 2 teaspoons garlic, chopped
  4. 1/2 teaspoon salt
  5. 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  6. 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  7. 2 teaspoons honey
  8. 2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice
  9. 2/3 cup parmesan cheese, shredded

Directions

  1. Shuck oysters and keep half shell on each (use an oyster knife or have a fishmonger shuck for you). Place oysters (in their half shells) on a baking sheet and keep them refrigerated until ready to use.
  2. Prepare honey-cheese topping by blending all the remaining ingredients in a medium bowl; mix well.
  3. Remove oysters from the fridge. Top each oyster with a heaping teaspoon of the honey-cheese mixture. Refrigerate oysters again until ready to grill.
  4. Preheat grill to high. Place oysters on the grill; cook for 10 minutes or until oyster shells begin to char and the honey-cheese topping is brown and bubbly.
  5. Using tongs, carefully transfer oysters from the grill to a serving tray. Serve immediately.

These grilled oysters offer a delightful combination of sweetness from honey, richness from Parmesan, and the briny goodness of fresh oysters. Adjust the quantities based on your preferences.
