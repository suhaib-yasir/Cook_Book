Chicken Curry

Embark on a culinary journey with the classic Chicken Curry, a dish that transcends cultural boundaries with its aromatic allure and rich flavor profile. Succulent pieces of chicken are lovingly simmered in a luscious blend of tomatoes, onions, and an array of spices. The curry, enriched with the warmth of ginger, garlic, and a secret medley of spices, invites you to savor a symphony of textures and tastes. The tender chicken, bathed in a flavorful sauce, becomes a delectable canvas upon which the essence of Indian spices paints a masterpiece. Whether paired with steamed rice or warm naan, Chicken Curry beckons, promising a culinary expedition that delights the senses and leaves an indelible mark on your palate.  

    

Ingredients

  1. Chicken pieces
  2. Onions, finely chopped
  3. Tomatoes, chopped
  4. Ginger-garlic paste
  5. Curry leaves
  6. Green chilies, slit
  7. Coriander powder
  8. Cumin powder
  9. Turmeric powder
  10. Red chili powder
  11. Garam masala
  12. Bay leaves
  13. Cinnamon stick
  14. Cardamom pods
  15. Cloves
  16. Coconut milk (optional)
  17. Coriander leaves, chopped (for garnish)
  18. Salt to taste
  19. Oil or ghee for cooking

Directions

  1. Heat oil or ghee in a pan. Add bay leaves, cinnamon stick, cardamom pods, and cloves. Sauté until fragrant.
  2. Add finely chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Stir in ginger-garlic paste and cook until the raw smell disappears.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft and the oil starts to separate.
  5. Introduce curry leaves, slit green chilies, coriander powder, cumin powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and garam masala. Cook the spices for a few minutes to enhance their flavors.
  6. Add chicken pieces to the spice mixture and sear until they are well-coated and slightly browned.
  7. Pour in enough water to cover the chicken. Season with salt and bring it to a boil. Reduce the heat, cover, and simmer until the chicken is cooked through and tender.
  8. If desired, add coconut milk for a creamier texture. Allow it to simmer for a few more minutes.
  9. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
  10. Serve hot with rice, naan, or bread of your choice.

Enjoy the delicious and comforting Chicken Curry! Customize the spice levels based on your preferences.
