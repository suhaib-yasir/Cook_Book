Prawn Puri

Indulge in the gastronomic delights of Indian street food with the enticing Prawn Puri. A canvas of golden, crispy puris awaits, delicately cradling a concoction of prawns marinated in an array of spices. The prawns, retaining their succulence, create a symphony of textures and flavors that transport you to the lively streets of India. With each bite, you embark on a culinary journey that captures the spirit of indulgence, where the crunch of the puri meets the delectable juiciness of perfectly spiced prawns.

Ingredients

  1. 2 tbsp rapeseed (canola) oil or ghee
  2. 1 star anise
  3. 5cm piece of cinnamon stick
  4. 1 onion, finely chopped
  5. 2 tbsp garlic and ginger paste
  6. 1 green bird's eye chilli, finely chopped
  7. 1/2 tsp Kashmiri chilli powder
  8. 1 tsp cumin
  9. 125ml (½ cup) tomato purée
  10. 700ml base sauce, heated
  11. 450g (1lb) small peeled raw prawns (shrimp)
  12. 2 tomatoes, quartered
  13. 1 tsp kasoori methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  14. 2 lemons (1 quartered)
  15. Salt, to taste
  16. 4 homemade puris or fried chapattis
  17. 3 tbsp finely chopped coriander (cilantro)

Directions

  1. Heat the oil in a large frying pan or wok over a medium–high heat. Add the star anise and cinnamon, let spices infuse for about 30 seconds.
  2. Add chopped onion, fry for 5 minutes until soft. Stir in garlic, ginger paste, and chilli; cook for another minute.
  3. Add Kashmiri chilli powder, cumin, tomato purée, and 250ml (1 cup) of base curry sauce. Bring to a rolling simmer, stirring only if sauce catches to the pan.
  4. Add prawns and another 250ml (1 cup) of base sauce, simmer until prawns are pink and almost cooked through. Adjust sauce thickness with more base sauce if needed.
  5. Stir in quartered tomatoes. Add kasoori methi, squeezing it between your fingers into the sauce.
  6. Squeeze juice of one lemon into the sauce, season with salt.
  7. Serve by placing one puri or fried chapatti on each plate. Divide prawn curry, garnish with chopped coriander, and a lemon wedge.

Enjoy the delightful combination of crispy puris and spiced prawn masala in this Prawn Puri dish! Adjust the spice levels according to your preference.
