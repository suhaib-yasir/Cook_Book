Hakka Noodles

Hakka Noodles, with their vibrant medley of colorful vegetables and perfectly cooked noodles, offer a riot of textures and flavors. The wok-kissed strands are entwined with an aromatic blend of soy sauce, ginger, and garlic, creating a symphony of savory goodness. The crisp-tender vegetables add a refreshing crunch, while the noodles absorb the essence of the seasonings. Hakka Noodles are not just a dish; they are a celebration of the wok's magic, delivering a quick and satisfying journey to the heart of Asian street food.

Ingredients

  1. Hakka Noodles
  2. Mixed vegetables (carrots, bell peppers, cabbage)
  3. Onions, thinly sliced
  4. Soy sauce
  5. Chili sauce
  6. Garlic, minced
  7. Oil
  8. Salt to taste

Directions

  1. Cook Hakka noodles according to package instructions, drain, and set aside.
  2. Stir-fry garlic and onions in oil until golden brown.
  3. Add mixed vegetables and saut√© until tender-crisp.
  4. Toss in cooked noodles, soy sauce, chili t.sauce, and salt. Mix well.
  5. Serve hot.

Enjoy the flavorful and colorful Hakka noodles! Adjust the sauce quantities and spice levels according to your preference.
