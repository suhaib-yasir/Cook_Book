Momos

Momos, those delicate pockets of culinary delight, beckon you into the enchanting world of dumplings inspired by the Himalayan landscape. These steamed or fried treasures cradle within them a flavorful filling of minced meat or vegetables, intricately seasoned with aromatic spices. Picture the first delicate bite, where the tender embrace of the dough gives way to an explosion of savory goodness. Served alongside a zesty dipping sauce, momos transcend mere snack status; they are an edible expedition that invites you to savor the essence of the mountains in each delectable fold and bite.  

    

Ingredients

  1. 2 cups all-purpose flour
  2. 1 cup water (for dough)
  3. 200g minced chicken or vegetables
  4. 1/2 cup finely chopped cabbage
  5. 1/4 cup finely chopped onions
  6. 1/4 cup finely chopped carrots
  7. 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  8. 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  9. 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  10. Salt and pepper to taste
  11. Oil for greasing
  12. For the dipping sauce:
  13. 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  14. 1 tablespoon vinegar
  15. 1 teaspoon chili sauce
  16. 1 teaspoon sugar
  17. Chopped spring onions for garnish

Directions

  1. Prepare the dough by mixing all-purpose flour and water. Knead until it forms a smooth, elastic dough. Cover and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, prepare the filling by combining minced chicken or vegetables with chopped cabbage, onions, carrots, soy sauce, sesame oil, ginger-garlic paste, salt, and pepper.
  3. Divide the dough into small portions and roll each into a thin, round sheet.
  4. Place a spoonful of the filling in the center of each dough sheet, then fold and pleat to form momos.
  5. Steam the momos in a greased steamer for 15-20 minutes until cooked through.
  6. For the dipping sauce, mix soy sauce, vinegar, chili sauce, and sugar. Garnish with chopped spring onions.
  7. Serve the hot momos with the dipping sauce and enjoy this delicious and comforting dish!

Enjoy the delicious and steamed momos! Experiment with different fillings and adjust the seasonings according to your taste preference.
