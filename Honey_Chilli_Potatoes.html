Honey Chilli Potatoes

Delight your taste buds with our irresistible Honey Chilli Potatoes – a culinary symphony that perfectly balances sweetness, heat, and crunch. Indulge in the crispy golden goodness of perfectly fried potato wedges, generously coated in a tantalizing blend of honey and chilli sauce. Each bite is a burst of flavor, with the sweetness of honey complementing the fiery kick of chillies, creating a harmonious dance on your palate  

    

Ingredients

  1. 4 medium-sized potatoes, cut into thin strips
  2. 1/4 cup cornflour
  3. 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  4. 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  5. Salt to taste
  6. Oil for frying
  7. 2 tablespoons oil
  8. 1 tablespoon finely chopped garlic
  9. 1 tablespoon finely chopped ginger
  10. 2-3 green chilies, chopped
  11. 1/4 cup honey
  12. 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  13. 1 tablespoon red chili sauce
  14. 1 tablespoon tomato ketchup
  15. 1 tablespoon vinegar
  16. 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  17. Spring onions for garnish
  18. Sesame seeds for garnish

Directions

  1. Combine cornflour, all-purpose flour, ginger-garlic paste, and salt in a bowl. Coat potato strips in this mixture.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and fry the coated potato strips until golden brown. Set aside.
  3. In another pan, heat 2 tablespoons of oil. Add chopped garlic, ginger, and green chilies. Sauté until fragrant.
  4. Add honey, soy sauce, red chili sauce, tomato ketchup, vinegar, and black pepper. Mix well.
  5. Add the fried potato strips to the sauce. Toss until the potatoes are well-coated.
  6. Garnish with spring onions and sesame seeds.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy your Honey Chilli Potatoes!

Enjoy the sweet and spicy goodness of homemade Honey Chilli Potatoes! Adjust the honey and chili levels according to your preference.
