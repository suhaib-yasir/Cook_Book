Chicken 65

Prepare to be enchanted by the spicy allure of Chicken 65, a beloved South Indian delicacy that has conquered taste buds across the globe. This dish is a celebration of bold flavors, where bite-sized chicken pieces are marinated in a fiery blend of red chilies, curry leaves, and aromatic spices. Deep-fried to a crispy perfection, Chicken 65 is a symphony of textures — the crunchy exterior giving way to the succulent, spice-infused meat. Each piece is a flavor bomb that leaves an indelible mark on your palate, making you crave the next delightful bite.

Ingredients

  1. Chicken pieces
  2. Curd (yogurt)
  3. Red chili powder
  4. Turmeric powder
  5. Ginger-garlic paste
  6. Cornflour
  7. All-purpose flour
  8. Rice flour
  9. Curry leaves
  10. Green chilies
  11. Coriander leaves
  12. Lemon juice
  13. Soy sauce
  14. Red food coloring (optional)
  15. Salt to taste
  16. Oil for frying

Directions

  1. Clean and wash the chicken pieces.
  2. In a bowl, mix curd, red chili powder, turmeric powder, ginger-garlic paste, and salt to make the marinade.
  3. Marinate the chicken pieces in the mixture and let it sit for at least 1-2 hours.
  4. In a separate bowl, mix cornflour, all-purpose flour, rice flour, and a pinch of salt.
  5. Coat each marinated chicken piece in the flour mixture, ensuring even coverage.
  6. Heat oil in a pan for deep frying.
  7. Fry the coated chicken pieces until they turn golden brown and crispy. Drain excess oil on a paper towel.
  8. In another pan, heat a little oil and sauté curry leaves, green chilies, and finely chopped coriander leaves.
  9. Add lemon juice, soy sauce, and red food coloring (if using) to the sautéed mixture.
  10. Toss the fried chicken pieces in this sauce until well-coated.
  11. Serve hot, garnished with additional coriander leaves.

Enjoy the spicy and flavorful Chicken 65 as a delicious appetizer or snack! Adjust the spice levels according to your preferences.
