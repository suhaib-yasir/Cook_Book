Mutton Marag

Immerse yourself in the opulent world of Mutton Marag, a regal soup that hails from the culinary traditions of the Indian subcontinent. This aromatic broth, simmered with tender mutton, is infused with a harmonious blend of spices, including saffron, cardamom, and cloves. The slow-cooking process allows the flavors to meld, creating a rich and soul-satisfying experience. Served hot, Mutton Marag is not merely a dish but a culinary tapestry that transports you to a realm of royal indulgence. With each spoonful, the nuanced flavors unfold, inviting you to savor the heritage and grandeur encapsulated in this exquisite creation.  

    

Ingredients

  1. Mutton - 500g
  2. Onions - 2, finely sliced
  3. Green chilies - 4, finely chopped
  4. Ginger-garlic paste - 2 tbsp
  5. Yogurt - 1/2 cup
  6. Coriander powder - 1 tbsp
  7. Red chili powder - 1 tsp
  8. Turmeric powder - 1/2 tsp
  9. Cloves - 4
  10. Cardamom - 4
  11. Cinnamon stick - 1 inch
  12. Bay leaves - 2
  13. Black pepper - 1 tsp, crushed
  14. Poppy seeds - 1 tbsp, soaked
  15. Cashews - 10-12, soaked
  16. Almonds - 10-12, soaked and peeled
  17. Fresh coriander leaves - for garnish
  18. Mint leaves - for garnish
  19. Ghee - 2 tbsp
  20. Salt - to taste

Directions

  1. Heat ghee in a pot, add sliced onions, and sauté until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies, sauté for a couple of minutes.
  3. Add mutton pieces and brown them on all sides.
  4. Add coriander powder, red chili powder, turmeric powder, cloves, cardamom, cinnamon, bay leaves, and crushed black pepper. Mix well.
  5. Whisk yogurt and add it to the pot. Stir continuously to avoid curdling.
  6. Make a paste of soaked poppy seeds, cashews, and almonds. Add this paste to the pot and mix well.
  7. Add salt and enough water to cover the mutton. Cover and cook on low heat until the mutton is tender.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and mint leaves before serving.

Mutton Marag is often enjoyed with naan or crusty bread. Enjoy the rich flavors of this comforting soup! Adjust the spice levels according to your preferences.
