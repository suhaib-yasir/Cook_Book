Squid Curry

Delight your taste buds with the exotic allure of Squid Curry, a culinary masterpiece that transforms tender squid into a spicy sensation. Simmered to perfection in a fragrant blend of curry spices, including cumin, coriander, and mustard seeds, the squid absorbs the rich flavors, creating a dish that balances the ocean's freshness with the warmth of Indian-inspired spices. Every mouthful promises a burst of flavor, making Squid Curry a must-try for seafood enthusiasts seeking a taste adventure.

Ingredients

  1. Coconut Oil - Â¼ cup
  2. Garlic - 1 tblsp chopped finely
  3. Ginger - 1 tblsp chopped finely
  4. Curry leaves a handful
  5. Green chillies - 2 chopped finely
  6. Onion - 1 large chopped finely
  7. Tomato - 2 chopped finely
  8. Chilli Powder - 2 tsp
  9. Kashmiri chilli powder - 1 tblsp
  10. Coriander powder - 1 tblps
  11. Cumin Powder - 2 tsp
  12. Turmeric powder - 1 tsp
  13. Garam masala powder - 1 tsp
  14. Black Pepper powder - 1 tsp
  15. Salt to taste
  16. Squid - 500 grams

Directions

  1. Heat coconut oil in a pan
  2. Add in chopped garlic, ginger, onions, curry leaves, and green chillies
  3. Saute for 2 mins or so
  4. Add in tomatoes and mix well
  5. Add in turmeric powder
  6. Add in chilli powder and mix well
  7. Add in coriander powder, cumin powder, and garam masala powder
  8. Add in salt
  9. Add in squid and mix well
  10. Cover and cook until squid is cooked completely
  11. Add in black pepper powder

Enjoy the delightful taste of Squid Curry with its rich and aromatic flavors. Adjust the spice levels according to your preference.
