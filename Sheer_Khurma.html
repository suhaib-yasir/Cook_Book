Sheer Khurma

Sheer Khurma, an epitome of culinary opulence, graces festive tables with its regal presence. This traditional dessert, deeply rooted in Islamic traditions, unfolds as a symphony of flavors and textures. As delicate vermicelli dances in a pool of sweetened milk, infused with the fragrant warmth of cardamom and embellished with saffron strands, Sheer Khurma takes on a celestial aura. The addition of dates and an abundance of dry fruits elevates this dish to a divine experience, symbolizing the richness of celebration and the warmth of communal gatherings.

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup thin vermicelli (seviyan)
  2. 1/4 cup ghee or unsalted butter
  3. 1 liter whole milk
  4. 1/2 cup condensed milk
  5. 1/2 cup sugar (adjust to taste)
  6. 1/4 cup chopped dates
  7. 1/4 cup chopped almonds
  8. 1/4 cup chopped pistachios
  9. 1/4 cup chopped cashews
  10. 1/4 cup raisins
  11. 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  12. A pinch of saffron strands soaked in warm milk
  13. Rose water (optional)
  14. Chopped dry fruits for garnish (optional)

Directions

  1. Chop dates, almonds, pistachios, cashews, and any other dry fruits you'd like to use.
  2. Soak saffron strands in warm milk and set aside.
  3. In a pan, heat ghee or unsalted butter. Add thin vermicelli and roast until it turns golden brown.
  4. In a separate pot, bring whole milk to a boil. Stir occasionally to prevent it from sticking to the bottom.
  5. Once the milk boils, add the roasted vermicelli. Cook until the vermicelli is soft and fully cooked.
  6. Stir in condensed milk and sugar. Adjust the sweetness according to your taste.
  7. Add chopped dates, almonds, pistachios, cashews, and raisins. Mix well.
  8. Pour in the saffron-soaked milk along with the strands and add cardamom powder. Stir to combine.
  9. Let the Sheer Khurma simmer on low heat for about 10-15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld and the dry fruits to become soft.
  10. Adjust the consistency by adding more milk if needed. Sheer Khurma should have a creamy and slightly thick texture.
  11. If using rose water, add a few drops at this stage for a fragrant touch.
  12. Garnish with chopped dry fruits and serve the Sheer Khurma warm.

Enjoy your delicious Sheer Khurma! It's a delightful dessert with a rich, creamy texture and the goodness of dry fruits. Adjust the quantity of sugar and dry fruits according to your taste preferences.
