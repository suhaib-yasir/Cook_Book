Gulab Jamun

A popular Indian sweet, Gulab Jamun consists of deep-fried dough balls, soaked in sugar syrup. These soft and spongy balls are flavored with cardamom and often served warm.

Rasgulla

A Bengali sweet, Rasgulla is made from chhena (paneer or cottage cheese) balls, which are boiled in sugar syrup. It results in spongy, light, and sweet balls that are often served chilled.

Rasmalai

Another delightful Indian dessert, Rasmalai consists of flattened chhena balls soaked in sweet, saffron-infused milk. It is garnished with pistachios or almonds, providing a rich and creamy texture.
