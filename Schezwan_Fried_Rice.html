Schezwan Fried Rice

A culinary marvel that marries the richness of Chinese fried rice with the bold and piquant allure of Schezwan flavors, Schezwan Fried Rice is a testament to the artistry of fusion cuisine. Fragrant basmati rice, wok-tossed to perfection, becomes a canvas for the vibrant dance of Schezwan sauce, crisp vegetables, and your choice of succulent protein. The result is a harmonious blend of spice, fragrance, and texture that elevates the humble fried rice to an irresistible delight, beckoning you to experience a culinary adventure with every delectable mouthful.

Ingredients

  1. Cooked rice
  2. Mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, bell peppers)
  3. Schezwan sauce
  4. Soy sauce
  5. Garlic, minced
  6. Spring onions, chopped
  7. Oil
  8. Salt to taste

Directions

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add garlic, and saut√© until aromatic.
  2. Add mixed vegetables and stir-fry until tender.
  3. Stir in Schezwan sauce, soy sauce, and cooked rice. Mix well.
  4. Garnish with chopped spring onions and serve.

Enjoy the spicy and flavorful Schezwan fried rice! Adjust the spice levels according to your preference.
