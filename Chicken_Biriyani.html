Chicken Biriyani

Embark on a gastronomic adventure with our delectable Chicken Biryani, a masterpiece of fragrant basmati rice, succulent chicken pieces, and a harmonious blend of aromatic spices. Each mouthful unveils a symphony of flavors, where the tender chicken and perfectly cooked rice are intricately woven together with our secret blend of spices, creating a dish that is both soul-satisfying and irresistibly delicious.  

    

Ingredients

  1. Chicken into pieces 1 kgs
  2. Rice 1 kgs
  3. Onion 6 nos
  4. Tomatoes chopped 3 big sized
  5. Potato peeled and halfed ( optional) 2 nos
  6. Ginger garlic paste 4 tsp
  7. Yogurt whipped 1 cups
  8. Ghee/oiL 1 cups
  9. Biryani masala 2 tbsp
  10. Kashmiri red chilli powder 1 tsp
  11. Garam masala powder 1 tsp
  12. Nutmeg and mace powder or paste 1 tsp
  13. Lemon juice 2 tsp
  14. Coriander leaves chopped 1 cups
  15. Mint leaves chopped 1 cups
  16. Green chillies pieces into slice 8-10 nos
  17. Kesar few threads soaked into hot milk with one tsp sugar
  18. Kewra essence( mix in kesar n milk mixture) 2-4 drop
  19. Fried onion 1/2 cup
  20. Whole garam masala-green black cardamom, black cumin or shahi Zeera, cloves, cinnamon sticks, star anise, bay leaves, black pepper 1/2 tsp

Directions

  1. Make ghee or oil hot and fry the onion until golden brown, add chicken and fry for few minutes on high flame
  2. Add ginger garlic paste n fry few minutes, add biryani masala, Kashmiri red chilli powder, salt and fry for 2 minutes. Add some water and cook on low flame until the chicken is tender.
  3. Add yogurt, garam masala powder n nutmeg n mace powder. Then increase heat and stir fry until oil separates from the gravy.
  4. Add tomatoes n cook few minutes ( don't mash or cooked tomatoes)
  5. Separately_ In 24 glass of hot water add 6 tbsp salt, lemon juice, whole garam masala some oil and soaked rice. Boil until the rice is more than half cooked or 80% cooked. Removed from heat and thoroughly drain the water.
  6. In a pot spread 2tsp ghee and then spread the boiled rice, after that spread the cooked chicken on the rice, then spread coriander leaves, mint leaves, green chillies and kesar or kewra essence milk. Make one or two layers of rice and chicken. Set the pot on a tawa. Cover n cook on low flame untill the rice is completely tender. Mix before serving.
  7. Serving suggestions-Garnish with fried onion or boiled egg.
  8. Served with raita n salad

Enjoy the delicious and aromatic Chicken Biryani! Adjust the spice levels and garnishes according to your taste preferences.
